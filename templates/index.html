ABOUT US






TrustRoot Network (abbreviation: TrustRoots) is a global platform facilitating trust-based partnerships between funders & grassroot organisations globally, by providing visibility and peer review opportunities - with the aim of creating a more localised, equitable, efficient and impactful aid sector. TrustRoots was incubated in 2021 as part of the Amani Institute’s Post-Graduate Diploma in Social Innovation Management, which both founders Julia Wegner and Pratiksha Jawanjale were part of. Since June 2022 the project is part of the Athari Group Accelerator Program.

Financial Inequity: Financial imbalances between donors and grassroots. Meaning only 1.2% of global humanitarian aid goes directly to local organisations
Low Effectiveness: Limited access to core and flexible funding prevents grassroots from sustainable implementation in their community’s best interest
Power Imbalance: Prioritisation and decision making power are still too often held by donors, rather than local actors and their communities
Pratiksha Jawanjale and Jey Wegner are experienced development professionals who met during their post-graduate diploma in Social Innovation Management at Amani Institute. Both entered the program asking why the so-called development sector was ineffective in creating changes - and a bond was struck. In 2021 they founded TrustRoots, a project aiming at promoting and facilitating trust-based partnerships between funders and grassroots organisations.

OUR JOURNEY


June 2021

Incubation: TrustRoots takes root as part of the Amani Institute’s Post-Graduate Diploma in Social Innovation Management.

September 2021

First Prototype: Our journey begins with the development of the first prototype.

June 2022

Athari Group Accelerator: TrustRoots becomes part of the Athari Group Accelerator Program, expanding its horizons.

September 2022

Funding Award: TrustRoots secures a funding award from Athari (Kyndral), marking a significant milestone.

August 2023

Wireframe Creation: The blueprint for TrustRoots takes shape with the creation of a wireframe for platform development.

November 2023

User Research: We delve into user research, gaining valuable insights to shape the TrustRoots experience.

December 2023

Tech Team Onboarding: Our tech team joins the journey, bringing expertise to turn vision into reality.
